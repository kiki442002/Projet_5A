STM32CubeMX Software V6.5.0 / 24-February-2022
Major release
  • Added the support for the STM32U59xxx and STM32U5Axxx new devices.
  • Added the support for the STM32C011xxx and STM32C031xxx new devices.
  • Added the support for two new part numbers in the STM32F7 Series.
  • Added the support for LPBAM firmware for the projects without Arm TrustZone (TZEN=0) in the STM32U575/585 product line.
