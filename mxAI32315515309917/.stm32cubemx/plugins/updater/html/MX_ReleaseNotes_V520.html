STM32CubeMX Software V5.2.0 / 19-April-2019
Major release
  • Added the support of new dual-core devices in the STM32H7 Series.
  • Added the support of devices with 64 Kbytes of Flash memory in the STM32G0 Series with examples in STM32CubeMX format available in the STM32Cube_FW_G0_1.2.0 STM32CubeG0 MCU Package.
  • Added the support of the STM32G4 Series with first examples in STM32CubeMX format available in the STM32Cube_FW_G4_1.0.0 STM32CubeG4 MCU Package.
  • New toolchain support: STM32CubeIDE.
  • Integration of the Cross Selector tool.
